How many days of leave is a electrician entitled to in a year?
11:31 AM
The annual leave an electrician is entitled to is 28 days of leave. This does not include leaves such as:
  1. Parental Leave
  2. Compassionate Leave
  3. Sick Leave

What is the cut-off in applying for unfair dismissal?
11:31 AM
According to the Fairwork Commission, an employee has 21 days from the date their dismissal took effect in which to lodge an application for an unfair dismissal remedy.
